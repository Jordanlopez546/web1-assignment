Ibrahim Ayo Balogun

Known professionally as Wizkid, a Nigerian singer and songwriter who has redefined Afrobeats and taken it to the global stage.

Biography

Wizkid, born Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun on July 16, 1990, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and global music sensation. He grew up in Surulere, Lagos, and began his musical journey at a young age, singing in his church choir. Wizkid rose to prominence in 2010 with his breakthrough single "Holla at Your Boy" from his debut album, Superstar (2011). He is known for blending Afrobeats, reggae, dancehall, and R&B, which has made him one of Africa's biggest musical exports.
